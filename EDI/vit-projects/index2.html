Relevance of Projects in VIT

SDP (Software Development Project)

SDP helps students learn end-to-end software development including planning, coding, testing, teamwork, and documentation following industry-like practices.

EDI (Engineering Design & Innovation)

EDI focuses on creativity and innovation. Students develop prototypes, apply design thinking, and solve real-world engineering problems.

DT (Design Thinking)

Design Thinking projects help students understand user needs, brainstorm ideas, and create simple and effective solutions through an innovative approach.

Course Projects

Course projects allow students to apply the concepts learned in subjects to real applications, improving understanding and practical knowledge.
